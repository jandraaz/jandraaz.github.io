Contimix | Spring 2015

With limited storage areas in today's homes, consumers are constantly drawn to products that transform, reducing the space they require and maximizing the space in the home. The Rubberduck Tub is a compressible baby tub perfectly designed with these living conditions in mind while ceasing to compromise the quality of the product.
The tub, made of rubber, compresses flat for easy storage. The supportive exterior ring, made of a rigid plastic, has groves and indents that provide the baby with a better and safer sitting posture while keeping the baby from sliding into the water. Thus, allowing the caregiver to relax and enjoy the bath time experience. The base of the tub benefits the caregiver too, as it provides a nearby location to set bottles or other bathing equipment. Additionally, the bottom of the base has rubber grips that keep the tub from moving, tilting, or falling. Furthermore, the head rest provides the baby with additional support and has a foam-like pillow that takes the shape of the baby's head.
With ever reducing living spaces in today's world, the Rubberduck Tub saves families' valuable space in their home and offers a high quality experience for both the baby and the caregiver.
