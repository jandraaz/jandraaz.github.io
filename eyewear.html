JANDRA ARANGUREN

Eyewear Design | Spring 2015

I've always thought that if my sister wore glasses, she would have the hardest time choosing them because she's the type of girl that can't wear silver earrings if she is wearing a golden necklace; she's the fashionista in the family. I decided I'd try to design glasses that she would wear if she ever had to.

I focused on incorporating versatility into the design and figuring out the way in which she could wear the same glasses every day without any problem. To accomplish this, the hinge the attaches the sides of the glasses to the actual frames is a strong magnetic one, allowing her to replace the sides with whatever matching color but also providing a strong and secure attachment to the frames.
