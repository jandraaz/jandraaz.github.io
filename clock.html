JANDRA ARANGUREN

Graphic Clocks | Spring 2015

Frankly, I'm not much of a clock person. I always found it a bit funny that people invest in watches and clocks when they all carry their phones around and when they want to check the time, they check their phone. So, I wanted to design a set of clocks that, if need be, could still be used to tell time, but were mainly designed as a decoration piece. I wanted to turn a clock into a piece of art that I would actually want to hang in my wall.

I searched for inspiration in minimal clock design and landed on a very graphic, sleek, modern, and very black-and-white aesthetic. I modeled it up as my first project using SolidWorks and prototyped it out of laser cut acrylic.
