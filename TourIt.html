TOURIT | Spring 2015

TOURIT intends to be a platform where stranded travelers can look for alternative ways to get home or tour the city, both with the option of connecting and sharing rides with other travelers. The app is broken down into four main parts:

  • 01. Notify My Friends

    Posts a message on the user’s Facebook and/or Twitter notifying their friends that they are stuck, in hopes that somebody may be in the area.

  • 02. Get Me Home

    Gives the user the option of connecting with other users and sharing a ride with them, or looking at buses and shuttles. In the case that “share a ride” is chosen, the user will be added to that destination’s chat and able to coordinate with other travelers. In the chat, the user can see who else is in the chat and has the ability to message them individually.

  • 03. Show Me The City

    Gives the user several attraction categories to choose from. Once chosen, a list of the open attractions is shown and the user can click to get more information. At each attraction’s page, the user will be able to see the open hours, get a description of the place, see a map, call, rate it, or share a ride. Like in Get Me Home, in the case that “share a ride” is chosen, the user is added to the attraction’s chat to be able to coordinate with other users.

  • 04. Store My Luggage

    Offers nearby places where travelers can safely leave their luggage while they visit the city.

On the Horizon:

- A better shell for smoother navigation. Currently, the user has to keep going back, which can very quickly get annoying. For a more enjoyable experience, a home button might be added to the shell.
- Test out the prototype with potential users.
