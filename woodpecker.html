The Ghost Bird | Fall 2015

Task:
Choose an extinct animal and design a symbol for it if it were to have existed in the National Zoo, at Washington DC.

The book below illustrates some of the research and processes that I did for this project. Beginning with analyzing the work done by Wyman and Cannan in their development of the National Zoo's symbol set and wayfinding system. Once the symbol characteristics had been identified I choose an animal - the Ivory Billed Woodpecker - and quickly got to work. I began with research about the animal, what it looked like, where it lived, what it ate, etc. and proceeded to mock up some quick illustrations for possible symbol forms. My overall process can be seen in the book below.
