Nepal Summer: Mahaguthi Craft With Conscience | Fall 2015:

This past summer, I spent 8 weeks in Nepal learning from and working hand in hand with Nepali artisans. The company I worked with was Mahaguthi: Craft With Conscience and our goal was to design and produce homegood products that are in line with Nepali tastes and also introduce something fresh to the market.
